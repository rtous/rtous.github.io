Retro 2D animations and sprites from reference video shots, enabling the iconic rotoscoped look of classics like Jordan Mechner’s Prince of Persia or Éric Chahi’s Another World.

Faster Workflows

Automate tedious frame edits and accelerate your reference-based animation workflow. Edit, segment, and stylize with precision.
